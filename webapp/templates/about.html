🎯 Research Objectives
  • 01 To examine existing approaches to traffic prediction, vehicle density detection, and route optimisation in the literature.
  • 02 To collect and preprocess traffic-related data suitable for training and evaluating machine learning models.
  • 03 To develop and train supervised machine learning models — Decision Tree and Random Forest — for vehicle density level estimation.
  • 04 To design and implement a graph-based route optimisation module that incorporates ML predictions to dynamically adjust routing decisions.
  • 05 To evaluate the performance of the proposed system using standard classification and routing efficiency metrics.
