How It Works
From Road Conditions to Optimal Route
Four stages that transform raw traffic data into a clear, congestion-aware route recommendation powered by machine learning.
Step 01 📍
Enter Journey
Select your source road, destination, and current time of day from the Route Query page.
Step 02 🤖
ML Prediction
Random Forest model (97.5% accuracy) predicts vehicle density — Low, Medium, or High — for every road segment.
Step 03 🗺
Route Optimisation
Dijkstra's Algorithm runs on a density-weighted road graph to find the least congested path.
Step 04 ✅
Best Route Output
System returns best route, congestion level, estimated travel time, and an alternative route for comparison.
🇳🇬 Nigeria's Traffic Challenge

Lagos is one of Africa's most congested cities. With over 21 million residents and a rapidly expanding vehicle population, the city's road network faces extreme pressure during morning (6am–10am) and evening (4pm–8pm) peak periods. Traditional navigation systems rely on static maps and do not account for real-time or predicted congestion levels.

This system addresses that gap by combining machine learning-based density prediction with graph-based route optimisation — providing a practical, cost-effective solution that requires no expensive sensor infrastructure.
